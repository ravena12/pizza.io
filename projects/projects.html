BusNapp

Tech: Swift

Sleep and never miss a stop again! This iOS app let's you create bus stops and set an alarm/distance for that bus stop. The user can create saved lists of the their favorite stops. When creating a stop the user can choose from a variety of songs, and distances. The app will track your location and when you reach that certain distance the app will wake you up!

Team: Charlotte Abrams and Raven Anderson

Timeframe: 4 days

Contribution: Push Notifications, Setup Audio, and Song List Views. Created main methods for taking users GPS location and creating alarm.
